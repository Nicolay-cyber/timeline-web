Welcome to Timeline

Timeline is a platform designed to simplify computing and modeling for individuals without programming skills but with understanding of mathematical concepts.

With Timeline, users can create models by manipulating mathematical formulas, without the need for writing code. Whether you're a scientist, engineer, or student, Timeline empowers you to explore complex mathematical relationships and visualize your data with ease.

Timeline allows you to share your models with the world, fostering collaboration and facilitating the exchange of scientific knowledge. This project aims to unite the scientific community by standardizing and unifying the data obtained from various processes.

Start creating your mathematical models today, share your insights, and contribute to the advancement of science!
